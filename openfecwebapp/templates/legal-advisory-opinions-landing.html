Advisory opinions are official Commission responses to questions about how federal campaign finance law applies to specific, factual situations.

Pending advisory opinion requests

Once the Office of the General Counsel has determined that an advisory opinion request meets all of the requirements to move forward for conisderation by the Commission, the request is made public and is available for public comment for ten days.
