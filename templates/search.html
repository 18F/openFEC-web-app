Get to Know the Data

Want to learn more about federal election data but aren’t sure exactly what you’re looking for? Start with a full set of data, then apply specific filters to find the information most relevant to you.

OpenFEC currently has financial data for all candidates for federal office and profile information for other committees involved in federal races, and we will be adding more soon.

Candidates

Candidates are people who are running or have run for office. Active candidates have at least one committee they have authorized to raise and spend money on their behalf in elections. If a person has been a candidate for multiple offices (for example, they ran for the Senate one year and President another year), they will have a candidate page for each office sought.

Browse all candidates »

Committees

There are many different types of committees involved in federal elections, and different types of committees have different fundraising and spending limitations and different requirements for reporting their activity. Committees can come in the form of an entity authorized by a candidate, a state or local political party, a non-party club or association or other group, and more.

About This Site

Welcome to OpenFEC, the Federal Election Commission’s new app for exploring campaign finance disclosure data. This site is in beta, which means we’re actively working on it and adding new features. The official site for FEC data is still the Campaign Finance Disclosure Portal.

Current Features

  • Search for candidates and committees
  • Browse and filter candidates and committees
  • View redesigned candidate and committee pages
  • View all of a candidate’s authorized and joint fundraising committees
  • View latest report
  • A public API

Coming Soon

  • View candidate reports, committee reports, and other filings
  • View and filter individual contributions, disbursements and other transactions
  • Download raw data and specialized datasets
  • Election pages
  • Campaign finance data visualizations

We're working hard to make OpenFEC a useful addition to the resources available at FEC.gov. Do you have feedback or feature requests? Email us and tell us what works well and what needs improvement.
