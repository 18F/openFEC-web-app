Get to Know the Data

Want to learn more about federal election data but aren’t sure exactly what you’re looking for? Start with a full set of data, then apply specific filters to find the information most relevant to you.

OpenFEC currently has financial data for all candidates for federal office and all committees involved in federal races, and we will be adding more soon.

Candidates

Candidates are people who have run for office. All active candidates have at least one committee they use to raise raise and spend money in elections. If a person has been a candidate for multiple offices (for example, they ran for senate one year and president another year), they will have a candidate page for each.

Browse all candidates »

Committees

Committees are the legal entities that raise and spend money in elections. They may be associated with one or more candidates; be backed by businesses, unions or other organizations; or be independent.

Browse all committees »
